Course 6: Educational Leadership and Change Management

← Back to Courses

Course Description

This course examines the theories and practices of effective leadership and change management within educational organizations. It focuses on developing the skills necessary to lead diverse teams, foster a culture of innovation, and navigate complex change processes in schools, universities, and other learning environments.

Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

  • Identify and apply various leadership theories relevant to educational contexts.
  • Analyze the dynamics of organizational change and resistance in educational settings.
  • Develop strategies for effective communication and stakeholder engagement during change initiatives.
  • Lead and manage diverse teams to achieve educational goals.
  • Design and implement change management plans for educational improvement.
