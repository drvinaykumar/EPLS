Course 7: Strategic Planning and Innovation in Education

← Back to Courses

Course Description

This course provides a deep dive into the principles and practices of strategic planning and fostering innovation within educational organizations. It equips students with the frameworks and tools necessary to analyze complex educational landscapes, develop forward-thinking strategies, and implement innovative solutions to address contemporary challenges.

Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

  • Understand the foundational concepts and models of strategic planning in education.
  • Conduct comprehensive environmental scans and stakeholder analyses for educational organizations.
  • Develop clear vision, mission, and strategic goals for educational initiatives.
  • Design and implement innovative solutions to complex educational problems.
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of strategic plans and innovation efforts.
