Course 8: EdTech Tools and Techniques

← Back to Courses

Course Description

This course provides a practical exploration of various educational technology (EdTech) tools and techniques that enhance teaching and learning. It covers a wide range of digital resources, platforms, and methodologies, focusing on their effective integration into diverse educational settings.

Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

  • Identify and categorize various EdTech tools and platforms.
  • Evaluate the pedagogical effectiveness and suitability of EdTech tools for different learning objectives.
  • Apply EdTech tools to design and deliver engaging and interactive learning experiences.
  • Understand the principles of digital citizenship, online safety, and accessibility in EdTech.
  • Integrate EdTech strategically to support diverse learners and promote inclusive education.
